LifeLine Phone Program offers free cell phone, free minutes, free texts

Budget Mobile LifeLine Info

Today, cell phones have become a part of everyday life for nearly everyone that lives in the United States.  Cell phones are used to conduct business, stay in touch with family members, and are used in the case of an emergency such as your car breaking down or having to call an ambulance.  

While we all need cell phones, not everyone can afford the expenses that accompany cell phones and the expensive plans that go along with them.  Budget Mobile offers a government assisted free phone program called LifeLine to help people who cannot afford a monthly cell phone plan. 

To qualify for a free government cell phone, you generally need to qualify for another government-assisted program such as supplemental Nutritional Assistance Program (SNAP), federal housing assistance, Medicaid, or others depending on the state you live in.  The LifeLine program is designed to help people who are having trouble paying their bills and reduce their monthly expenses.     

If you would like to learn more about how you can qualify for a free government phone and service through Budget Mobile, please visit our support page or get in touch with us through our social media channels on Facebook and Twitter.  We are always happy to answer any questions you have on free cell phone service through the government sponsored Lifeline program.
